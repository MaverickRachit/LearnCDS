# **App Name**: CDS English Ace

## Core Features:

- Static Vocabulary: Provides a fixed, offline library of 1000+ essential vocabulary words tailored for the CDS exam.
- AI Vocabulary Updates: Daily AI-driven updates that deliver new vocabulary words relevant to current defense news.
- Static Idioms & Phrases: Offers a collection of 500+ key idioms and phrases vital for CDS exam preparation, available offline.
- AI Idiom Generation: The app will incorporate AI to automatically fetch and contextualize new idioms and phrases on a daily basis.
- Adaptive Grammar Quizzes: AI generates grammar quizzes with questions dynamically adjusted based on the user's performance, creating a personalized learning path.
- Personalized AI Tutor: A chat-based AI tutor is included, allowing students to ask for specific help, such as generating vocab lists or explaining grammar rules. The LLM is designed to use reasoning (as a 'tool') to customize the user's study materials.
- Gamified Progress Tracking: Visual tools and progress tracking will reward points and badges for daily app engagement to incentivize the student

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Navy blue (#2E3192) for conveying trust and intelligence, nodding to defense-related themes without being overtly military.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#D2D3E6), offering a subdued backdrop that maintains readability and reduces eye strain.
- Accent color: Sky blue (#708090) will provide highlights on interactive elements, buttons and other important information in the app, and work together with navy blue
- Body and Headline Font: 'Inter', a grotesque sans-serif for clean readability on all devices.
- Crisp, professional icons complement learning categories. Consider scalable vector graphics for responsive clarity.
- Organize key sections into dedicated tabs, ensuring logical content navigation and easy information access.
- Employ micro-interactions to highlight completed lessons or showcase achievement badges to keep users motivated.
